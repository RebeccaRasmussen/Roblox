# Models

This folder is for storing references and documentation about 3D models used in your games.

## What are Models?

Models in Roblox are:
- 3D objects made of Parts, MeshParts, and Unions
- Can include scripts and other objects
- Can be saved and reused across games

## Creating Models

1. **In Roblox Studio**: Build with Parts, Unions, and MeshParts
2. **Group Objects**: Select multiple parts and press `Ctrl+G` to group
3. **Save to Inventory**: Right-click the model and "Save to Roblox"

## Finding Models

- [Roblox Creator Marketplace](https://create.roblox.com/marketplace)
- Build your own in Roblox Studio
- Use the Toolbox in Roblox Studio (Home tab → Toolbox)

## Tips

- Name your models descriptively
- Keep models organized in folders
- Test models before using in your game
- Be careful with free models - check scripts for safety
- Always give credit to creators when required
